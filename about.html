About Noeru Learning（ノエルラーニングについて）

Noeru Learning is a language and mathematics education platform designed for learners in Singapore.

Our unique approach comes from growing up in a multilingual environment. Unlike educators from single-language backgrounds, we understand the real challenges of learning Chinese or English as a second (or third) language. We use that understanding to build relatable, effective teaching materials for Singaporean students.

ノエルラーニングは「学びを進める」ことをコンセプトに、言語教育と数学教育を通じて、次世代の可能性を育みます。

We believe that learning should be relatable, encouraging, and built on empathy. Whether your child is aiming for improvement in Chinese, mastering English writing, or building a strong math foundation — Noeru Learning is here to support their journey.
